Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

A real notepad at the center, with AI that fills in everything you didn't have time to write.

A Real Notepad

Notes lead every meeting. Headings, lists, checklists, highlights, full keyboard shortcuts — and every line remembers when you wrote it. Click a margin timestamp and playback jumps to that moment.

Enhance My Notes

One tap rewrites your shorthand into real meeting notes, filling in specifics from the transcript. Your original stays one toggle away, with full revert.

Pro

Instant Live Transcription

Words appear on screen as they're spoken, transcribed on-device. Nothing leaves your Mac during the meeting — it even works offline.

Conference Mode

Capture your mic and the meeting's audio from Zoom, Meet, or Teams — no bot, no browser extension. Live channel meters confirm both sides are coming through.

Pro

Automatic Action Items

Every meeting gets action items with owners, priorities, and due-date hints. Send them to Apple Reminders or Todoist in one click.

Pro

Visual Notes

Turn any meeting into a 4K visual summary, bound into a sketchbook you can flip through. 7 styles, 4 aspect ratios, custom color palettes.

Pro

TL;DR-First Summaries

Summaries open with the TL;DR and decisions, every speaker identified automatically. Six built-in recipes — from Executive Brief to Follow-Up Email — or write your own.

Ask AI & Search

Ask questions about any meeting — even while it's still happening — and search across every transcript and note you've ever taken.

How It Works

Three steps. No learning curve.

Take the notes you'd take anyway. Tagalong turns them into the record of the meeting.

Capture

Tagalong notices your meeting starting and can begin recording automatically — or press Cmd+Shift+R. Conference mode captures both sides of the call.

Jot

Type shorthand in the notepad while live transcription runs on-device — every word on screen instantly, nothing leaving your Mac.

Stop

Speakers are identified, your notes are enhanced from the transcript, and a summary with action items is ready — all processed in the background.
