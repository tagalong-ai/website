Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Powerful AI working quietly from your menu bar so you can focus on the conversation, not the notes.

One-Click Recording

Click the menu bar icon. That's it. Tagalong captures your mic — or your entire conference call — without a bot joining the meeting.

Speaker Identification

Tagalong labels who said what automatically. Edit speaker names once and they carry through the transcript, summary, and infographic.

AI Summaries

Structured summaries with decisions, action items, and key takeaways. Choose from 4 built-in recipes or create your own template.

Visual Infographics

Drop a meeting infographic into your deck or Slack channel. 5 styles, 3 aspect ratios, 6 color palettes.

Conference Mode

Capture system audio from Zoom, Meet, or Teams alongside your mic. No browser extension needed.

Yes. Conference Mode captures system audio from any app on your Mac — Zoom, Meet, Teams, Webex, and anything else that plays sound. No browser extension or meeting integration required. You can also import recorded audio files (MP3, WAV, M4A, MP4, AAC) for post-meeting processing.
With a Private License, your API keys stay in the macOS Keychain and every request goes directly to the AI provider. No audio or transcript data passes through our servers. Your recordings are saved as markdown files in ~/Documents/Tagalong/ on your Mac. You own your data.
Tagalong requires macOS 15.0 (Sequoia) or later. It runs natively on both Apple Silicon and Intel Macs as a universal binary.
